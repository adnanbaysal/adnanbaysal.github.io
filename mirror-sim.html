Optics Lab v2.5

A precision simulator for billiard dynamics and reflective geometry.

Core Physics

Particles follow vector reflection laws. In the Ellipse, rays emitted from one focal point ($c = \sqrt{a^2 - b^2}$) will perfectly converge at the other. Use the Precision slider at high speeds to ensure particles don't tunnel through boundaries.

Interaction

Movement: Click anywhere on the canvas to move the light source. The cursor will automatically snap to the center, principal axes, and elliptical focal points for exact alignment.

Visual Snap Guides

Snap Proximity: Hover near the center or focal points. A target ring will appear when you are within snapping range.

Axis Alignment: Hover near the midline to see a preview dot on the axis. Clicking will lock the light source exactly to that line.

Focal Properties: Try placing the source exactly on a focal point. You'll see the light converge perfectly at the other focus!

Simulation Guide

Logarithmic Speed: Slider doubles the speed at each step (1, 2, 4, ... 16384).

Shape Control: Switch between Ellipse and Rectangle. 'a' controls the width.

Snapping: Hover near axes or focal points to see snap guides. Click to place the source precisely.

Heatmap: Glowing edges indicate where particles are hitting the boundary.

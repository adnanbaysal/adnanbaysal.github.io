Who am I?

Hello! I'm Adnan from Türkiye. I'm a mathematics enthusiast and software engineer. This is my portfolio of ideas that I believe worth sharing with others. My portfolio can be grouped under three types of content:

  • Most of them are tools that you can play with. I'm the main consumer of them so the UI is mostly for my taste.
  • Some of them are explainers of the math behind those tools.
  • And others are articles about anyhing I'm interested in and want to share.

Probably most — if not all — of the ideas behind these tools, explainers, and articles are reinventions of existing ideas, but some may have original parts in it.

The following are my core interests and the tools I used for the purpose of this "portfolio". I have other interests and use many other tools professionally, but they do not appear in the things I share here.
